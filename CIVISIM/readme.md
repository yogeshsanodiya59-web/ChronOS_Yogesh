# CIVISIM: A Civic-Policy Managing Tool

---

## CIVISIM: Origin

Implementing policies in a diverse and large population is complex.  
Cultural, regional, and socio-economic differences can cause a single policy to affect groups unevenly.

Often, unintended consequences arise due to limited foresight during early formulation stages.

**CIVISIM emerges from the need for structured pre-implementation evaluation** â€” not to criticize governance systems, but to strengthen informed decision-making.

---

## CIVISIM: Breakthrough

CIVISIM bridges the gap between policymakers and citizens by providing transparent, structured analysis of draft policies before implementation.

It enables visibility into:

- Intended impacts  
- Potential risks  
- Trade-offs  

This promotes informed awareness and accountability.

---

## CIVISIM: System Overview

CIVISIM allows officials to upload draft policy documents (`PDF` / `DOCX`).

The system extracts:

- Policy objectives  
- Impacted entities  
- Ambiguities  

It then runs scenario-based simulations to identify:

- Potential risks  
- Unintended effects  
- Trade-offs  

Instead of providing a single deterministic result, CIVISIM presents multiple possible outcomes for structured comparison.

## Scope and Limitations

CIVISIM is a **decision-support system**.

It does not replace human authority or guarantee predictions.  
It provides structured insights based on modeled assumptions to assist policymakers in making informed decisions.
